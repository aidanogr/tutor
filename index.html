Our Services

Group Lessons ($20/hour)

Weekly two hour sessions involving warm-up questions, lesson breakdowns and a main focus on key concepts, including hand-written practice problems to apply what we learned. Offered for ACCT 229 tutoring.

Private Sessions ($40/hour)

Personalized tutoring sessions to focus on the individual needs of students. This allows extra time for questions and additional explanation to comprehend difficult concepts. Offered for ACCT 229 tutoring.

Online Tutoring ($Variable)

For students who do not have transportation or simply do not prefer meeting in person, both group lessons and private sessions can be held online. Materials will be presented through Zoom. Offered for ACCT 229 tutoring.

About Aggie Business Tutors

Hi! I’m Brady Dinkel, the founder of Aggie Business Tutors. It sounds fancy, but simply put, I’m just one student helping another to succeed. I started tutoring ACCT 229 while I was in the midst of taking the course with friends who were having a bit of a tough time breaking down the content. It’s a fast-paced class with lots of stuff to cram in, so it can be challenging to keep pace. Beginning to tutor ACCT 229 was one of the best decisions I made my freshmen year. As a young entrepreneur, I have spent time trying various businesses; tutoring is the one my heart and soul is in. As a bonus, I’m just a nerd. Accounting makes sense to me and I enjoy it. I hope that after going through tutoring with Aggie Business Tutors, students will feel the same about ACCT 229 and business in general.
